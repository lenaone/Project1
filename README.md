# Tic Tac Toe

### Summary
Tic-tac-toe is a game for two players, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. There are two buttons for "play again" and "reset". When you click "play again", a new game starts and the score is not reset. When you click "reset", a new game starts and the score reset. 
Try it. Have fun with it.
